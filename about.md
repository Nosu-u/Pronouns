# Pronouns!
### If you want to contact me for whatever reason, message me on Discord (nos.uu) or open an issue on [Github](https://github.com/Nosu-u/Pronouns!)

Tired of people misgendering you in the daily chat? Say no more!

With this mod you'll be able to add the pronouns you use to your GD profile and will be able to see the pronouns of every other person that also has it installed.

You can put them and change the color of the text on the mod settings or by clicking the text on your GD profile, just in case you miss it.

Be proud, be woke <3
